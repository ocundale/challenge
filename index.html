Instructions

To begin the demonstration, play and pause the video on the left.

Every time you play a section of the video content, a 'fragment' is created and displayed in the output.

Each fragment consists of a start and stop time in milliseconds and defines the specific section of the video that was viewed.

These fragments may overlap, for example if you were to skip back and replay the first five seconds of the video multiple times.

Clicking the Calculate UVT button will process the recorded fragments to calculate and display the Unique View Time, showing the number of ms of unique content that has been seen by the viewer.

If you wish to input your own custom fragments, you may do so by clicking the button below.


Please input an array of fragment start/end times (ms) in the text box below.
For example: [[1223,2333],[2333,3444],[5000,6000]]
